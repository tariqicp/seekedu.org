What has SEEK been up to lately?

 

HandyInc., and S.E.E.K.

 

  1. S.E.E.K has offered to help Handy with their HAUTE Fashion Show fundraiser to pilot their STEM program.  If you would like to volunteer for this event, please email our Volunteer Coordinator- Ambry Johnson you contact information.
  2. In addition. we  will be working with Handy Inc., to produce and kick start their first  STEM program.  .

     

Breakthrough Miami and S.E.E.K
 

  1. S.E.E.K.  will continue their collaboration  with Breakthrough Miami throughout the school year for our STEM Program. The small group of girls will be creating their own organic, cosmetic face, hair and skin products. 
  2. S.E.E.K, will provide Character Education/Anti-bullying workshops for a group of teenage girls with Breakthrough Miami starting in June 2015.
  3. Donations for the S.T.E.M program can be made  through our website at www.seekedu.org

 

New process on how to volunteers for S.E.E.K
  1. S.E.E.K.,will start conducting mandatory orientation for all volunteers. Registration is available through our website at   http://www.seekedu.org/Volunteers.html
  2. Volunteers can confirm their attendance for each event through Eventbrite on our website at http://www.seekedu.org/Volunteers.html
  3. If you have not attend the orientation and you are a former volunteer, please read the volunteer manual (http://www.seekedu.org/Volunteers.html ), read and sign the Volunteer Affidavit,  Acknowledgement & Receipt, and Code of Conduct Certification and Disclosure.
     
Sponsorships/Donations
  1. All sponsorship packages for various events will be available on our website after February 10, 2015
  2. Tangible donations can be brought on the day of the event or 48 hours  prior to the event (contact Volunteer Coordinator - Ambry Johnson at ajohnson@seekedu.org).
  3. Contact the Executive Director for all sponsorships -Anike Sakariyawo | asakariyawo@seekedu.org
